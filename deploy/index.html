What's New

  • Sea Girt Lighthouse

    Come take a tour of the Sea Girt Lighthouse. This historic landmark hosted the first radio beacon mounted in a shore installation in the United States.

  • Sea Girt Lifeguards

    Want to learn about beach safety or about how to apply to become a lifeguard? Visit the Sea Girt Beach Patrol’s Website here, by clicking Learn More.

  • The Parker House

    Need to grab a bite to eat? Stop by the Parker House for some fine food and good times!

  • Tide Trackers & Tide Charts

    Come check out the hourly tide tracker and tide charts to see when it is the perfect time to go in the ocean!

  • Wildlife & Endangered Species

    Learn more about Sea Girt’s wildlife, as well as the beach’s endangered species. See where they will be nesting this summer.
